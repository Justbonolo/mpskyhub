About us.


Mpsky hub is a new digital agency company based in South Africa that provides businesses with services to boost their online presence across search engines and social media. Our ultimate mission is to help businesses expand, attract more clients, and successfully manage the challenges of online marketing by offering creative and effective solutions that are suited to their specific needs. This will help them stay competitive in the fourth industrial revolution era.

By offering consultations, graphic designs, managing websites and social media accounts, Mpsky hub seeks to establish a long-term partnership with its clients. We want to make sure that we give them the finest services possible, given that an online presence is essential for any kind of business.

Our history

In 2024, Bonolo Mpisekhaya, a University of Johannesburg alumnus and native of Johannesburg, established MpSky Hub. Upon completing a Harvard Online course, he started conceptualising an idea for this digital agency. His objective was to help anyone who might need support thriving in the digital era by using the knowledge and abilities he acquired from the programme.

Mission
  • To enable companies across South Africa by offering complete, customised web solutions that optimize their online presence.
  • To advise with different companies and discuss strategies to help them thrive in the online environment.
  • Our goal is to support their expansion, broaden their consumer base, and maximize their success in the digital era.
  • We work to be the driving force behind our clients' success by providing them with individualized support, state-of-the-art technologies, and creative tactics that will help them prosper in the rapidly changing context of the fourth industrial revolution.
Vision
  • To establish ourselves as the premier and most reliable partner for South African companies looking to succeed online. In our ideal future, every business, no matter how big or little, will fully utilise internet platforms to reach a wider audience, build relationships with them, and experience long-term success.
  • We hope to be the inspiration behind the online success stories of companies all around the country, ultimately advancing the digital economy in South Africa, through our dedication to innovation, knowledge, and a client-centric approach.
